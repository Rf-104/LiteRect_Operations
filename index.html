User Guide

沉浸式桌面收纳引擎
Minimalist Desktop Organization Engine
在 Microsoft Store 中获取 Get it from Microsoft Store

🚀 万物皆可拖拽

🚀 Drag & Drop Everything

无需复杂设置，将桌面上的任意文件、庞大的文件夹或浏览器网址直接拖入半透明网格中，LiteRect 会在底层自动且安全地为您生成快捷方式。

No complex setups. Drag any files, massive folders, or web URLs directly into the translucent grid. LiteRect safely generates shortcuts in the background automatically.

✨ 智能隐形与自由布局

✨ Smart Auto-Hide & Movement

您可以自由拖拽移动收纳框。开启“自动隐藏”后，鼠标移开，网格温柔隐入壁纸；鼠标滑过，瞬间浮现。它绝不打扰您的全屏游戏或观影体验。

Freely move grids anywhere. Enable "Auto-hide" to let the grid fade smoothly into your wallpaper when inactive, appearing only when you hover over it. Zero interruptions during gaming.

🎨 像素级个性化定制

🎨 Pixel-Perfect Customization

右键点击网格空白处，即可唤出原生设置菜单。支持 48px 到 96px 图标大小精细调节，支持每行 3 到 9 个图标的灵活排版，更可随心调节背景透明度。

Right-click on any empty grid space to access native settings. Adjust icon sizes (48px-96px), change grid layouts (3-9 per row), and tweak background transparency to match your aesthetic.

🗂️ 无限独立工作流

🗂️ Infinite Workspaces

在系统右下角托盘图标处右键选择“新建收纳窗口”，即可创建无限个整理空间。您可以为“游戏”、“工作”分别创建独立的收纳框，彼此物理隔离，互不干扰。

Right-click the tray icon to create new, independent windows. Set up isolated workspaces for "Gaming" or "Work" to build your ultimate desktop workflow without interference.
